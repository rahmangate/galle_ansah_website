About Dance
Dance with Galle(DWG)Offers dance enthusiasts, dancers, funs of Galle , and others who want to learn dance professionally, the forum to do so.
Dance with Galle offers Classes on African contemporary dance, Neo-Traditional dances, hip hop, popular dance, dance therapy(fitness classes) , choreography to all sexes, and ages.
Dance with galle also organizes Dance Workshops, Theatrical performances, complete art Workshops within Ghana, Africa and globally as well as provide dancers for music videos and TV commercials .
Our mission is to eventually expand to become an Art School, adding other fields of arts.
